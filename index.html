Vault Plus — Complete Guide
Everything you've unlocked with your subscription.
Every feature explained in detail.
1
Automatic price tracking
Product card
Vault automatically checks the price of every saved product each time you open its card. If the price changes from the original, the variation is highlighted directly on the card — green if the price drops, red if it rises.
Chronologically adds the current price to the product history. No action required from you.
2
Price drop alerts
Vault Plus in the sidebar
The Price Alerts section lists all saved products that have a recorded price. You can set a threshold: when the price drops below it, you receive a notification in the app status bar. Each product shows the current price, the starting price, and the percentage change.
Access via the Vault Plus button in the sidebar, then select Price Alerts from the Plus screen.
3
Advanced price history
Statistics
Inside the Statistics section you'll find a line chart showing price trends over time for each product. The chart is interactive: hover over points to see the exact price on that date. The timeline covers the full history from the day you added the product.
The history is built automatically every time the price changes. Products added without a price do not appear in the chart.
4
Advanced statistics
Statistics in the sidebar
Advanced Analytics unlocks detailed metrics: average spend by category, product distribution by price range, purchase rate (how many wishlist items get bought), products with the biggest savings vs original price, and a monthly purchase summary.
In the Free version this section appears locked. With Plus the lock disappears and real data becomes visible.
5
AI Taste Profile
Vault Plus in the sidebar
Analyses all saved products and builds a personal style profile. The system calculates: dominant price range (budget, mid-range, premium), favourite category, ratio of explored vs purchased products, starred favourites, average wishlist price, and conversion rate from "want it" to "bought".
The profile updates every time you open the panel. Requires at least 3 products in the vault for meaningful results.
6
AI suggestions
Coming soon
Based on your Taste Profile, the system will generate product suggestions consistent with your shopping style: same category, similar price range, recurring brands from your list. Suggestions appear as clickable cards that open directly to the product page.
Feature in active development — available in the next update for all Plus users.
7
Multiple workspaces
Coming soon
Create separate independent vaults: one for personal use, one for gifts, one for professional projects. Each workspace has its own sections, products, statistics and visual theme. Switch between workspaces with a single click from the sidebar without losing data.
Feature in development — each workspace's data is saved separately in local storage.
8
Cloud Backup
Vault Plus in the sidebar
Automatically syncs your vault to the cloud, making your data accessible from any device. The Cloud Backup panel shows how many items are in your vault and the sync status. Backup is incremental — only changes are sent, not the entire archive every time.
Currently being implemented on the backend. The panel shows the count of locally saved items.
9
Premium themes
Settings
Unlock exclusive colour palettes not available in the Free version: high-contrast monochromatic themes, warm and cool colour variants, white background with coloured accents, and seasonal themes. Each theme consistently modifies background, borders, text and accent colours across the entire interface.
Coming in the next version. The current light/dark theme remains available for all users.
10
Advanced mood boards
Mood Board view (layers icon)
The basic mood board view shows products as side-by-side images. The Plus version adds fullscreen mode with free drag-and-drop layout, the ability to add text labels and captions over images, manual card resizing, and export of the board as a PNG image.
The basic mood board view remains available to all. Advanced editing features activate automatically with Plus.
11
Priority access to new features
Automatic with Plus plan
Every new feature is released to Plus users weeks ahead of the Free version. This includes new online shop integrations, new item types, new views, and every interface improvement. You don't need to do anything: features appear directly in the app as soon as they're released.
Features marked with the Plus badge in the menu are exclusive previews for your plan.
