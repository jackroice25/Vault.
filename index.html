08 Questions

Good to know.

Is Vault really free?+
Yes. The free plan is free forever, save products and places from any site, organise them into sections, use the grid, list, globe and moodboard views, and sync privately across your devices. Vault Plus (€4.99/month) is optional and adds sharing, gift lists, reminders and more.
How does saving from a website work?+
Copy the link of anything you like, a product, a restaurant, a hotel, and paste it into Vault. It reads the page and fills in the title, image and price automatically. With Vault Plus you can also save the page you're on in one click using the browser extension.
Which websites are supported?+
Most of the web works out of the box, shops, marketplaces, Google Maps, Booking and countless others. A few large brands use aggressive bot protection that can block automatic reading; on those, Vault still saves the item with a clean name from the link, and you can add the photo and price yourself in a couple of seconds.
What exactly do I get with Vault Plus?+
Everything in Free, plus: shared & collaborative lists, gift mode with reservations, the one-click browser extension, savings goals, smart reminders, AI insights into your taste and spending, advanced moodboards and premium themes. See the Plus section above for the full breakdown.
Can I share a list with other people?+
Yes, with Vault Plus. Shared lists let you invite anyone and edit together in real time, ideal for couples, families or a group trip. Gift lists go a step further: share one link (no account needed to open it) and friends quietly reserve what they'll buy, so nobody gifts the same thing twice.
Is my data private?+
Completely. There are no ads and no tracking. Your data is yours, passwords are securely hashed, everything travels encrypted, and you can export all of it to CSV or delete your account at any time. Read our Privacy Policy for the details.
Do I need to install anything?+
No. Vault runs in your browser, nothing to download. You can use it as a guest with everything stored locally, or create a free account to sync across devices. Native desktop and mobile apps are on the way.
